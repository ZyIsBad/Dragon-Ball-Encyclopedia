About Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is a Japanese media franchise created by Akira Toriyama in 1984. The story follows the adventures of Goku from his childhood through adulthood as he trains in martial arts and explores the world in search of the seven orbs known as Dragon Balls, which summon a wish-granting dragon when gathered. Along the way, Goku makes several friends and battles a wide variety of villains, many of whom also seek the Dragon Balls for their own desires.
